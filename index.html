Strategic Leader in Business Transformation & Enterprise Technology

I am a Business Process Consultant and Transformation Leader with more than 18 years of international experience helping organisations design strategies, execute large-scale change, and realise measurable business value enabling clients to navigate complexity and achieve sustainable growth.

In parallel, I have delivered training, workshops, and executive coaching in Europe, South Africa, the Middle East, India, and the Americas, equipping leaders and teams with the skills to drive lasting change.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between business ambition and execution, guiding organisations through complex transformations — whether in strategy, operations, technology, or people.
